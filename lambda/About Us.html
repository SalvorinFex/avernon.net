Company history

I started this enterprise in July 2009 to help out those computer users who are having issues that are keeping them from having a great experience with their PC. 

My name is Kurt Roesener and I have over 10 years experience with computer trouble-shooting and I will be glad to look into why your computer isn't behaving.

I have worked at such places as Sony, Intuit Software, University of San Diego and The UpperDeck Company and Nokia.  I have learned the bulk of my knowledge by getting hands on experience in demanding roles from Helpdesk Support, System Integrator and Technical Support Specialist.  I will put that knowledge to work for you.
